011-初识ChIP-seq

Posted on 2023-03-18 Edited on 2023-03-19 In ChIP-seq

我是上周开始接触ChIP-seq的。

师兄说，首先要了解一下ChIP的实验怎么做，以及各种实验方法的侧重点和优缺点。然后，是ChIP-seq分析的pipeline,以及各种常用的算法。之后，就拿一些数据实操一下。

然而，由于对刘小乐老师由衷的喜爱，我先去看了她的STAT115 Module2关于ChIP-seq的公开课。课程内容些庞杂（不仅仅是ChIP-seq相关的实验方法和分析,还有许多关于epigenetics的内容及算法），大致如下：

Transcription Factor Motif Finding：

  • Sequence Logo
  • Known motif scans from Jaspar, HOMER, FactorBook or Hocomoco
  • De novo motif finding: Positive weight matrix update;Expectation maximization: MEME; Gibbs Sampler
  • Use conservation and motif modules (redundant and combinatoriamotif in poximity) to improve signal to noise

Transcription Factor ChlP-seq:

  • MACS for peak calling
  • QC: PBC,FDR, # of peaks with > FC, FRP, DHS overlap, conservation, motif enrichment
  • TF-TF interactions:
    • Motif enrichment: HOMER, Cistrome
    • ChlP-seq peak overlap: Cistrome DB Toolkit Giggle search
  • TF target genes:
    • Regulatory potential(# and distance)
    • Could weight by epigenetic signal
    • Could consider TAD boundaries or HiC
    • Differential expression
    • Function: GO/GSEA

Epigenetics:

  • DNA methylation and demethylation
  • Nucleosome positioning(Mnase-seq)

Hidden Markov Models
